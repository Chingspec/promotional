INTERACTIVE EDUCATIONAL TOOLS

SeaSource offers a variety of interactive educational tools to engage users and promote a deeper understanding of seafood sustainability:

Seafood Trivia

Test your knowledge of seafood species, sustainable fishing practices, and culinary techniques.

Match the Seafood

A matching game to identify different seafood species and their sustainable alternatives.

Sustainability Quiz

Assess your understanding of environmental and social impacts of fishing practices.

EDUCATIONAL RESOURCES

SeaSource offers a variety of educational resources to empower individuals and organizations to make informed decisions about seafood consumption and production. These resources include:

Informative Articles and Blog Posts

A beginner's course covering the basics of seafood sustainability, including overfishing, bycatch, and habitat destruction.

Interactive Tools and Calculators

An advanced course for industry professionals on sourcing sustainable seafood, supply chain management, and traceability.

COMMUNITY FORUM

SeaSource's community forum is a vibrant online space where seafood enthusiasts, students, educators and community groups can connect, share knowledge, and collaborate. The forum provides a platform for:

Discussion & Debate

Engaging in discussions on various seafood-related topics, such as sustainable fishing practices, seafood traceability, and marine conservation.

Knowledge Sharing

Sharing insights, tips, and experiences related to seafood sourcing, preparation, and consumption.

Peer-to-Peer Learning

Learning from other members of the community through Q&A sessions, workshops, and online courses.

Networking Opportunities

Connecting with like- minded individuals and organizations to build relationships and collaborate on projects
